About LocalPals

A new layer between
guests and local life

LocalPals is a concierge network built for travelers staying in private accommodation. Guests access a curated guide of trusted local services — all recommended through their host, powered by LocalPals.

We're now building our founding partner network in Zagreb and Istria — recruiting both property partners and experience providers who care about exceptional guest experiences.

How It Works

Three steps to a better guest stay

01
Guests access LocalPals

On arrival, guests receive access to LocalPals through their accommodation — a curated local concierge guide, personalised for their stay.

02
They discover & request

Guests browse trusted local experiences — restaurants, wine tours, private chefs, wellness, transfers — and request with ease.

03
LocalPals connects them

We coordinate with our partner network to confirm, handle logistics, and ensure a seamless experience from first request to final delivery.
